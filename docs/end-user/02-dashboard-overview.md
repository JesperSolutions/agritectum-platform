## âš™ï¸ Customizing Your Dashboard

Make your dashboard work for your workflow:

### Rearrange Widgets

1. Click **"Customize Dashboard"** (pencil icon in top-right)
2. **Drag widgets** to your preferred positions
3. **Drop them** where you want them
4. Click **"Save Layout"**

### Show/Hide Widgets

1. In customize mode, click the **eye icon** on any widget
2. Hidden widgets can be restored from the sidebar
3. Your changes save automatically

### Reset to Default

1. In customize mode, click **"Reset to Default"**
2. Confirm the action
3. All widgets return to their original positions
