## ğŸ›‘ Cancelling an Agreement

To cancel an active agreement:

1. **Open the agreement** details
2. **Click "Cancel Agreement"**
3. **Select reason** for cancellation
4. **Choose cancellation date:**
   - Immediate (may incur early termination fee)
   - End of current billing period (recommended)
   - End of agreement term (if near expiration)
5. **Review any penalties** or refunds
6. **Confirm cancellation**

### Cancellation Policy
- **Notice period:** Usually 30-60 days (check your terms)
- **Early termination:** Fee may apply (typically 1-3 months' cost)
- **Prorated refund:** For annual billing, unused months may be refunded
- **Scheduled visits:** Completed visits are still charged

## ğŸ†˜ Common Questions

**Q: Can I pause an agreement temporarily?**
A: Contact support. Some agreements allow seasonal pauses for seasonal properties.

**Q: What happens if I sell a building during the agreement?**
A: Remove the building via "Modify Agreement". Billing adjusts accordingly.

**Q: Can I transfer an agreement to a new building owner?**
A: Contact support. Transfer is possible with both parties' consent.

**Q: Do agreements cover emergency repairs?**
A: Standard agreements cover inspections only. Emergency services may be available at additional cost or via separate agreement.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with service quality?**
A: Contact support immediately. Document issues and request service improvement or cancellation.

**Q: Are price increases allowed?**
A: Check your terms. Most agreements allow annual CPI-based adjustments with 60-day notice.

**Q: Can I have multiple agreements for the same building?**
A: Yes, for different services (e.g., roof monitoring + ESG reporting).
