### How do I get access to the portal?
You'll receive an invitation email from Agritectum. Click the link and follow the registration process.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. Go to https://agritectum-app.web.app/portal
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Create new password

### Can multiple people access my account?
Yes! Contact support to add team members with different permission levels.

### How do I change my email address?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Account Settings ‚Üí Update email. You'll need to verify the new email.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256), secure servers in the EU, and comply with GDPR regulations.

## üè¢ Buildings

### How many buildings can I add?
No limit! Add as many properties as you own or manage.

### Can I add buildings in different countries?
Yes. The system supports international addresses and currencies.

### What if my building address doesn't appear in the search?
Type the full address manually. Some map features may be limited without geocoding.

### Can I organize buildings into groups?
Not currently, but you can use filters and search to manage large portfolios. Custom tags are coming soon.

### How do I measure my roof size on the map?
When adding/editing a building:
1. Enter and select the address
2. Click "Measure" next to roof size
3. Click points around the roof perimeter on the map
4. Double-click to finish
The area calculates automatically.

### Can I bulk import buildings?
Not yet, but it's planned. Contact support if you have 50+ buildings.

## üìä Inspections & Reports

### How often are buildings inspected?
Depends on your service agreement:
- Standard: Annual inspection
- Professional: Semi-annual (twice per year)
- Enterprise: Quarterly (four times per year)

No agreement? Inspections are scheduled as-needed.

### When will I receive my report?
- **Draft report:** Within 3-5 business days after inspection
- **Final report:** Within 7 business days
You'll be notified via email and dashboard when reports are ready.

### Can I request a specific inspection date?
Yes, when confirming appointments, you can propose alternative dates if needed.

### What happens if critical issues are found?
You'll be notified immediately (phone call + email) for safety/structural issues. Non-urgent issues appear in the report.

### Can I download reports as PDF?
Yes. Open any report and click "Download PDF" in the top-right corner.

### How long are reports stored?
Indefinitely. All historical reports remain accessible in your account.

## üí≥ Billing & Payments

### What payment methods are accepted?
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfer (manual or automatic via invoice)
- Direct debit (coming soon)

### When am I billed?
Depends on your billing frequency:
- **Monthly:** 1st of each month
- **Quarterly:** 1st of each quarter
- **Annually:** On agreement anniversary

### Can I change my billing frequency?
Yes. Go to Billing ‚Üí Manage Subscriptions ‚Üí Change billing frequency. New frequency applies at next renewal.

### What happens if my payment fails?
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Automatic retry in 3 days
3. If still failing, update your payment method
4. Service continues for 14 days during resolution
5. Agreement suspends if not resolved within 30 days

### Do you offer discounts?
- **Annual billing:** 5-10% discount vs. monthly
- **Volume:** Discounts for 10+ buildings
- **Multi-service:** Bundle pricing available
Contact sales for custom quotes.

### Can I get invoices for accounting?
Yes. All invoices are available in Billing ‚Üí Invoice History. Download as PDF for accounting.

### Is VAT/sales tax included?
Prices shown exclude VAT. Tax is added based on your country and registered for VAT.

## üîß Troubleshooting

### I can't log in
- Verify email and password
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if still blocked

### Building photos won't upload
- Check file size (max 5MB per image)
- Ensure format is JPG, PNG, or HEIC
- Check internet connection
- Try a smaller batch (5 at a time)

### Dashboard is loading slowly
- Check internet speed
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito/private mode
- Report persistent issues to support

### Notifications aren't working
- Check browser notification permissions
- Verify email preferences in Profile
- Check spam folder for emails
- Ensure notification settings are enabled

### My question isn't answered here
Contact support or check the detailed guides in the documentation.

## üìä Data & Privacy

### Who can see my building data?
- **You:** Full access to all your buildings
- **Agritectum staff:** Only assigned inspectors and support
- **Team members:** If you've added them to your account
- **No one else:** Data is private and encrypted

### Can I export my data?
Yes. Contact support to request:
- Building database export (CSV/Excel)
- All reports (PDF bundle)
- Complete account data (GDPR compliant)

### How do I delete my account?
Contact support to request account deletion. All data will be permanently removed within 30 days (GDPR right to erasure).

### Do you sell my data?
Absolutely not. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

### Where is data stored?
Secure servers in the EU (Denmark/Germany). Compliant with GDPR and EU data protection laws.
