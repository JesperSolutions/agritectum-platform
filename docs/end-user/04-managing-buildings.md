## ðŸ“ Measuring Roof Size on Map

If you don't know the exact roof size:

1. **During building creation or editing**, look for the address field
2. **Enter and select the address** from dropdown
3. **Click "Measure" button** next to roof size field
4. **On the map:**
   - Click points around the roof perimeter
   - Double-click to close the shape
   - The area is automatically calculated
5. **Click "Save Measurement"**

The roof size will be filled in automatically!

> ðŸ’¡ **Tip:** Use satellite view (toggle in top-right of map) for better roof visibility

## ðŸ“¸ Managing Building Photos

### Adding Photos

**During creation:**
- Drag & drop images into the photo area
- Or click "Browse" to select files

**After creation:**
1. Open building details
2. Click "Edit Building"
3. Scroll to Photos section
4. Click "+ Add Photos"

### Photo Best Practices
- **Front elevation:** Full building facade
- **Roof view:** From ground or drone if available
- **Problem areas:** Close-ups of damage or issues
- **Surroundings:** Context photos showing access, adjacent buildings
- **Interior:** If relevant (for commercial properties)

### Organizing Photos
- Drag to reorder (first photo is the cover photo)
- Click "Set as Cover" on any photo
- Click "Delete" to remove
- Add captions/descriptions

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Use descriptive names:** "Warehouse 3, Copenhagen" better than "Building 3"
2. **Add photos immediately:** Easier to remember details when first adding
3. **Measure roof accurately:** Affects cost estimates for service agreements
4. **Update year built:** Helps with maintenance planning and depreciation
5. **Add access notes:** Save time for inspectors ("Key in lockbox, code 1234")
6. **Tag locations:** Use consistent naming for multi-building sites
7. **Regular photos:** Take new photos annually to track deterioration

## ðŸ†˜ Common Questions

**Q: Can I bulk import buildings from a spreadsheet?**
A: Currently no, but this feature is planned. Contact support for large portfolios (50+ buildings).

**Q: What if the address isn't found in autocomplete?**
A: Type the full address manually. Map features may be limited without geocoding.

**Q: Can I merge duplicate buildings?**
A: Contact support to merge buildings and preserve historical data.

**Q: How do I transfer a building to another account?**
A: Contact support. They'll handle the transfer and maintain inspection history.

**Q: Why can't I delete a building?**
A: Buildings with active service agreements or scheduled visits must be resolved first.

**Q: Can multiple users manage the same building?**
A: Yes, if they're part of the same organization account.
