Inspector Detailed Guide

Getting Started

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Complete your profile setup
  3. Download the mobile app (PWA)
  4. Test offline functionality

Creating Reports

  1. Navigate to "Reports" → "New Report"
  2. Enter customer information
  3. Select inspection type
  4. Complete inspection checklist
  5. Add photos and notes
  6. Review and submit

Best Practices

  • Always verify customer information
  • Take clear, well-lit photos
  • Document all findings thoroughly
  • Use offline mode for remote locations
  • Sync data when connection is available

Troubleshooting

  • Report won't save: Check offline status
  • Photos not uploading: Reduce file size
  • GPS not working: Enable location services
  • App crashes: Clear browser cache

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Contact your branch administrator or superadmin to reset your password. They can generate a new temporary password for you.

Can I work offline?

Yes! The application is designed to work offline. Create reports, manage customers, and schedule appointments without internet connection. Data syncs automatically when online.

How do I install the mobile app?

Visit the application URL on your mobile browser and look for the "Install App" option in your browser menu. This installs a native-like app on your device.

Who can I contact for support?

For technical issues, contact your branch administrator first. For system-wide problems, contact the superadmin or system administrator.
